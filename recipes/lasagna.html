How to make Lasagna

This is my recipe; first are the ingredients, next come the directions.

Ingredients

  • one box of pasta
  • one jar of canned tomato
  • minced meat or substitute
  • ricotta or dairy free substitute

Directions

  1. first cook meat
  2. next remove meat and cook the sauce
  3. now, while cooking sauce, cook the pasta to the point where it's almost fully cooked and limp, but still somewhat raw
  4. mix the meat and the sauce
  5. now get a casserole dish and start layering the sauce, then pasta, then ricotta, and repeat. Once finished, cover with cheese
